# Causal Steering

This section focuses only on intervention behavior.

## Why it matters

Transfer overlap alone is not enough.  
Steering tests whether learned concept directions produce controllable effects.

## Final Observations

- Set-ConCA intervention curves show consistent gain under stronger intervention scale.
- Weak-to-strong transfer behavior remains one of the strongest narrative elements.
- Random directions behave as expected control (degradation / weaker alignment).
